Dozer(Twix)

Say hello to a 7-month-old Beabull -- a Beagle and Bulldog mix. He is a VERY sweet boy who was surrendered by his owners who didn't have the time to care for him. He has recently been fully vetter and neutered. Twix gets along with kids, dogs, people; cats are unknown but he's such a mush he probably be fine with them.

If you are interested in fostering or adopting one of our dogs, please use the button above to download an adoption application and then email the completed document to bffdogrescuenj@gmail.com. We will review all applications within two to three days of receival. We require a veterinarian and reference check before adoption. Our adoption fee helps to cover our expenses and includes all shots that the dog is old enough to receive, fecal check, spay/neuter, and any other fees required when we rescue. The adoption fee for puppies is $350, adult dogs are $300 and older/senior dogs fees are evaluated on a individual basis.
